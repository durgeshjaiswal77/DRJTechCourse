Data Entry Operator

A Data Entry Operator is a professional who is in charge of entering all the data into different computer databases. In addition, they manage and maintain effective record keeping, organizing files to collect information for future use.

A Data Entry Operator compiles and verifies data to ensure accuracy while appropriately formatting it. This includes preparing documents for entry and transcribing from paper formats into computer files using manual entry or scanners.
